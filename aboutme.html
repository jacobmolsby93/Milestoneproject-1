My story

How I Got Into The Developer Industry.

I got into this industry by always having a passion for creating and loving the technical aspect of things. When I was studying to get my commercial pilot license I started to play around with coding. I think it is safe to say I fell in love straight away.


How I Am Learning

After looking around for several months to find a school that suited my options. I found a course online, The Code Institute. Now I am on the way to become a Full-Stack Developer in a time frame that suits me perfectly. The Course is great and the more I learn I realize that the course goes through the whole spectrum of developing Web-Applications.


My Goals For The Future.

My goal after completing this course is to start my business and get into Freelancing as a Web-Developer.
